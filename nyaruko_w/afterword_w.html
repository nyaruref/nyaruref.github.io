Afterword W


Hey all, lig0schndr here.

"Haiyore! Nyaruko-san W" has come to an end, and blogging about it has been just as fun and interesting as last time. Once again I want to thank everyone who supported this blog. I feel that it's thanks to everyone's interest in Nyaruko that this blog is a success. I truly appreciate all the help and support that everyone has shown. Thank you all so much! I hope that this blog is a useful source of information about the references and parodies in "Haiyore! Nyaruko-san W", and I hope that this blog helps people enjoy the series more.

Once again I would like to thank Steman and servitors. They both collected and explained many references on their blogs. Steman's blog has a lot of information with good explanations. servitors' blog specializes in Cthulhu Mythos related content and is very detailed. I learned a great deal from reading their blogs and most of the information on this blog I learned from their blogs. Thank you both so much. Also, I want to thank the users at the "Haiyore! Nyaruko-san Moto Neta Wiki" (這いよれ！ ニャル子さん 元ネタwiki). This wiki is a great source of information, as well. It even has references from the light novel series.

If there is any reference that I missed or anything that needs correction, please point it out to me and I'll address it as soon as possible. Thanks!

Everyone, thanks so much for reading. Let's meet again!
