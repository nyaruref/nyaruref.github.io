Haiyore! Nyaruko-san W Opening and Ending References


References in the opening:
The poses done by the characters in the opening are based on the transformation poses of various Kamen Riders, super heroes in a series of Japanese television shows called "Kamen Rider" (仮面ライダー). Additionally, most of these poses are poses previously used on the covers of the "Haiyore! Nyaruko-san" light novel. Post for reference:
https://nyaruref.github.io/nyaruko/nyaruko_light_novel_covers.html


Nyaruko is mimicking Kamen Rider Fourze from "Kamen Rider Fourze" (仮面ライダーフォーゼ), the twenty second "Kamen Rider" show in the "Kamen Rider" series. It aired from 2011 to 2012.


Nyaruko is mimicking Kamen Rider 1 from "Kamen Rider" (仮面ライダー), the first show in the "Kamen Rider" series. It aired from 1971 to 1973. Additionally, Nyaruko makes this pose on the cover of volume 2 of the light novel. Image for reference:

The game system that Nyaruko and Cthuko are playing with is based on the Mega Drive video game console equipped with the Mega CD and Super 32X attachments.S2 The Mega Drive, also known as the Sega Genesis, was released by Sega in 1988. The Mega CD (Sega CD) was released in 1991 and added a CD-ROM drive to the Mega Drive. The Super 32X (Sega Genesis 32X) was released in 1994 and increased the capabilities of the Mega Drive. There are two references here:
* The pose Nyaruko does here is referencing the same pose she does on the cover of "GA Bunko Magazine Vol.1" (GA文庫マガジン Vol.1), a magazine published by GA Bunko on January 12, 2012. Image for reference:

Source of image: https://bookwalker.jp/de14aad71e-cfbc-4331-8eaa-574850851750/
* Here is an isolated image of the text on the screen:

The Japanese text that appears in this shot goes as follows, "Meijou shigatai seisaku iinkai no you na mono" (名状しがたい製作委員会のようなものW), which translates to "Unspeakable production committee-like thing W". The word "unspeakable" (名状しがたい meijou shigatai) is an adjective that often appears in the Cthulhu Mythos.
